About the game

The game was released on April 27, 2021, by DaeriSoft. It was developed by two schoolchildren, Kwon Oh-Jun and Heo Jae-Ho.

The Spike: Volleyball Story is a two-dimensional sports game that combines elements of casual gameplay with a charming anime art style. It offers a unique and enjoyable experience for volleyball fans and casual gamers alike. In the game, players take on the role of a young volleyball player who aspires to become a champion. The narrative gameplay traces the path of the protagonist from novice to experienced athlete, showing various challenges, training, and exciting volleyball matches. The game successfully captures the essence of the sport, providing an exciting and engaging volleyball experience.

History of the game


The launch of the game began on March 24, 2018 by Osik and Funya.


March 24, 2018 is the day that The Spike finally saw the light of day. The launch date kept getting postponed like an unattainable dream, receding forever and pushing back until the very end. And now, in the wee hours of Monday morning, the long-awaited moment has arrived - The Spike is finally before the world.

Without consulting the Daerisoft team, they boldly announced, "We're releasing it on March 24!" And although the decision was made without DaeriSoft's advice, a few days later they had already given the official cafe - The Spike, albeit with flaws and bugs, but still.

A lot has happened since then. The Spike has become famous and brought the developers a lot of connections. They've really accomplished a lot. At the end of his junior year of high school, Osik was given the important task of running The Spike. And he still remembers how he struggled with it. Even in college, he was constantly thinking about it. What he means is that he definitely struggled with the backlash.

There was always a sense that he should perform because so many people expected him to. However, he had watched YouTube videos about The Spike, he had seen The Spike wiki tree being created, he had read it with Funya. He knew that he had created a game that some people had put dozens of hours into, some had lost, some had actually dismantled, and some had just kept as a memory. To him, it was the ultimate happiness.
He was on TV, he met with journalists, he made so many connections because of The Spike.
